Introduction

ICT4M 2018 is a conference that gathers academicians, researchers, practitioners, professionals, and students to address the challenges of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) for the Muslim world. This conference has been conducted six times so far with the most recent location in Jakarta, Indonesia on 22-24 November 2016.

For this upcoming event, ICT4M 2018 will be conducted on 23-25 July 2018 in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. The theme for this year is “Sustainable Technologies for Islamic Digital Economy”.

It will be hosted by the Kulliyyah of Information and Communication Technology, International Islamic University Malaysia (KICT, IIUM) and co-organized by the IIUM Institute for Islamic Banking and Finance (IiBF).

All accepted papers will be submitted for indexing in IEEE Xplore. Selected papers will be invited for publications in the following journals (subject to the requirements of the respective journals):

  1. Journal of Cases on Information Technology (IGI Global)
  2. ASM Science Journal (Akademi Sains Malaysia)
  3. International Journal on Perceptive and Cognitive Computing (IIUM)
  4. Journal of Islamic Finance (IIUM)
